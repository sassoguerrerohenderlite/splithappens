Split Happens

Family Law Insights with Sasso Guerrero & Henderlite – Jacksonville, FL

Our Story

Split Happens was created by the team at Sasso Guerrero & Henderlite to open the conversation about family law beyond the courtroom. As one of Jacksonville’s most respected family law firms, our attorneys understand that divorce, custody, and co-parenting are not just legal issues—they are deeply personal experiences. With this podcast, we share clear insights, practical guidance, and meaningful stories that help people navigate the complexities of Florida family law with confidence. Our mission is to provide clarity, support, and perspective during some of life’s most challenging transitions.

About the Podcast

Hosted by meteorologist Katie Garner and Board Certified Family Law Attorney Jay Henderlite, Split Happens doesn’t shy away from the tough conversations—divorce, custody battles, emotional breakdowns, and everything in between. Each episode explores real issues faced by families while also offering perspective, humor, and hope. With Jay’s legal expertise and Katie’s engaging, relatable approach, the podcast makes complicated topics like alimony, time-sharing, and property division easier to understand. Whether you’re facing a family law case or simply want to learn more about the process, Split Happens delivers trusted insights in a fresh, approachable format.

About the Hosts

Jay Henderlite is a Board Certified Family Law Attorney who has spent years representing clients in divorce, custody, and other complex family law cases in Florida. His deep knowledge of the law and ability to break down difficult legal topics make him a trusted voice for anyone seeking clarity during a challenging time. Katie Garner, a seasoned meteorologist and communicator, brings warmth, humor, and empathy to the conversation. Together, Jay and Katie create an engaging space where listeners can learn, reflect, and find encouragement as they navigate family law issues.

Who Should Listen

Split Happens is for anyone impacted by family law and the challenges that surround it. The podcast speaks to parents navigating custody arrangements and the realities of raising children across two households, spouses facing the financial and emotional complexities of divorce, and individuals seeking to better understand their rights under Florida family law. It also resonates with friends and family members who want to support loved ones through the process. By combining expert legal guidance with open, honest conversation, Split Happens offers clarity, encouragement, and hope to anyone touched by separation, divorce, or custody disputes.

About Sasso Guerrero & Henderlite

Sasso Guerrero & Henderlite is a premier family law firm based in Jacksonville, Florida, serving clients across Northeast Florida. Our team is led by three Board Certified Marital & Family Law Experts, the only firm in the region with this distinction, ensuring unparalleled knowledge, skill, and dedication in every case.

At SGH, we are family lawyers—and families ourselves. We raise children, foster children, adopt children, and understand the profound stakes involved in protecting and supporting families. This personal experience informs everything we do, from divorce and child custody cases to adoption, assisted reproductive technology (ART) law, LGBTQ+ family matters, and all aspects of family law.

Our firm combines legal expertise with a premium client experience. We are passionate advocates who guide clients through complex and emotional situations with care, clarity, and professionalism. When you choose Sasso Guerrero & Henderlite, you are working with a team committed to protecting your family and helping you navigate some of life’s most challenging transitions with confidence.
